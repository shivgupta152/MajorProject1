Symptoms

COVID-19 affects different people in different ways. Most infected people will develop mild to moderate illness and recover without hospitalization.

Symptoms of Coronavirus

High Fever

  • fever/high temperature (37.8C or greater)
  • loss of, or change in, sense of smell or taste

Cough

  • have 3 or more episodes of coughing in 24 hrs
  • are coughing more than usual

Sore Troath

  • a dry, scratchy and redness in throat
  • a painful throat, especially when swallowing

Headache

  • pain in the eyes when looking into lights
  • nausea, dizziness, vertigo

Stay at home and call your doctor: Seek immediate medical attention if you have serious symptoms. Always call before visiting your doctor or health facility.
