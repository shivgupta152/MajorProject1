Covid-19 Awareness

Wear a mask.Save lives.

Stay aware of the latest information on the COVID-19 outbreak, available on the national,state and local public health authority

How to prevent

Stay at home

The risks of getting COVID-19 are higher in crowded and inadequately ventilated spaces where infected people spend long periods of time together in close proximity

Wear facemask

Make wearing a mask a normal part of being around other people

Keep social distancing

Maintain at least a 1-metre distance between yourself and others to reduce your risk of infection.

Wash your hands

Regularly and thoroughly clean your hands with an alcohol-based hand rub or wash them with soap and water

How to Prevent Coronavirus?

If COVID-19 is spreading in your community, stay safe by taking some simple precautions, such as physical distancing, wearing a mask, keeping rooms well ventilated, avoiding crowds, cleaning your hands, and coughing into a bent elbow or tissue. Check local advice where you live and work. Do it all!

  • Avoid the 3Cs: spaces that are closed, crowded or involve close contact.
  • Avoid touching your eyes, nose and mouth
  • Cover your mouth and nose with your bent elbow or tissue when you cough or sneeze
